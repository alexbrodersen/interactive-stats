6 Ethics

Given that we are collecting data from humans, there are additional ethical concerns with how data are collected, transmitted, and stored. Severe penalties can be imposed for failure to abide by these guidlines, and as such it is important to be well versed in them if you will be handling data collected in our lab.

6.1 CITI Training

The Collaborative Institutional Training Initiative (CITI Program) is an organization that provides learning materials and educational courses that focus on “research, ethics, regulatory oversight, responsible conduct of research, research administration, and other topics pertinent to the interests of member organizations and individual learners”.

From the CITI website:

These materials are designed and regularly updated to:

  • Enhance the knowledge and professionalism of investigators, staff, and students conducting research in the United States and internationally
  • Educate members, administrators, and leadership of ethics committees that review and oversee research
  • Promote ethical research at organizations through the education of research administrators and organizational leadership

Notre Dame has an institutional license its members to obtain training provided by CITI. To access it, do the following:

  1. Click this link: CITI Training
  2. Click “Log In” on the top right.
  3. Click “LOG IN THROUGH MY INSTITUTION” at the top.
  4. Scroll down to select “University of Notre Dame”
  5. Follow the required steps (If applicable).

You will only be required to compete the Social & Behavioral Research under the Human Subjects Research section. This can be found by clicking “View Courses” and then “Add a Course”.
